    • Description

    You can make sure that your indoors are cool and comfortable at all times with the help of the Whirlpool Magicool Air Conditioner. Its 6th Sense Intellicool technology, which combines smart sensors and advanced algorithms, can automatically manage temperature and humidity with the push of a single button. This way, it gives you the optimal cooling atmosphere. This air conditioner, which uses Brushless Direct Current motor fans, features smart speed regulation and high-torque operation for increased air circulation. Moreover, this air conditioner ensures long-lasting performance with little noise because it functions with almost no friction.

    Product Description
    Optimal Performance

    With the touch of a single button, the Whirlpool Magicool Air Conditioner's exclusive 6th Sense Intellicool technology along with a combination of smart sensors and sophisticated algorithms can automatically control the temperature and humidity. This way, it provides you with an ideal cooling environment

    BLDC Fans

    Built with Brushless Direct Current motor fans, this air conditioner offers smart speed regulation and high-torque operation for high air circulation. Besides, as it operates with almost zero friction, this air conditioner ensures enduring performance with minimal noise.

    High Cooling

    Even when the ambient temperature is as high as 52°C, this air conditioner can keep your space cool and comfortable.

    Self-clean Function

    Integrated with an inbuilt self-clean function, this air conditioner can remove moisture and dust buildup inside the door unit, preventing the growth of dangerous microbes, harsh odours, and rust on the coil.

    Stabiliser-free Operation

    Functional with a wide voltage range from 140 V to up to 285 V, this air conditioner offers optimal protection from voltage fluctuations. And, it delivers consistent cooling even at low voltage.

    Gas Leak Indicator

    Equipped with a gas leak indicator, this air conditioner won't be running with low gas levels.

    Hidden Display

    Featuring an attractive design and a hidden display, this air conditioner is an excellent addition to your contemporary home.

    HD Filter

    Sporting a high-density HD filter, this air conditioner ensures that the air in your space is clean and hygienic.

    Intellisense Inverter

    Boasting Intellisense Inverter technology, this air conditioner manages your electricity usage. And, its sophisticated inverter technology adapts to your cooling requirements while using less energy and maintaining low power consumption.

    Copper Build

    As the indoor evaporator unit, outdoor condenser unit, and all connecting tubes and pipes are designed using copper, this air conditioner performs well and lasts a long time.

    R32 Eco Refrigerant

    Leveraging R-32 refrigerant, this air conditioner has a low Global Warming Potential (GWPC). Additionally, with this refrigerant's high energy efficiency, this air conditioner uses low refrigerant volume and produces low amounts of greenhouse gases when operating. Moreover, since it is not a mixture of several other refrigerants, the eco-friendly R-32 refrigerant can be recycled with ease.

    Capacity Convert

    With the 'Convert' button on your remote, you can effortlessly switch between four different tonnage capacities to meet your needs.

    Stabilizer Free Operation

    The stabiliser-free operation protects your appliance from power surges and allows for continuous cooling even at low voltages.
